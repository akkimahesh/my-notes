1. for every pod liveness, readiness and startup probe is not same.

starup probe:
application is started?
note: until startup probe succeed readiness and liveness not start...

readiness:
application is start and functionalities is working or not..

liveness:
application is providing respose or not.

another example:
Startup Probe → "Has the application finished starting?"
Readiness Probe → "Can the application receive traffic?"
Liveness Probe → "Is the application still alive?"

real life example:
starup probe:
bike is started?

readiness:
bike is started but it is able to move?

liveness:
bike is started and also it able move, but currently it is moved or stoped?

example:
=======
startupProbe:
  httpGet:
    path: /health/startup
    port: 8080
  periodSeconds: 10
  failureThreshold: 12

meaning:-
Check every 10 seconds

12 failures allowed

12 × 10 = 120 seconds
